Operating Your Smart Switch Device

To connect a new device:

  1. If you don't have a SmartSwitch account please Register Here first.
  2. Press the touch sensor for 5 seconds until it starts blinking.
  3. Connect to the device by phone/computer by selecting "SmartSwitch ..." on the WiFi selection settings of your phone/computer.
  4. Open your browser of choice.
  5. Go to 192.168.4.1.
  6. Choose a WiFi network for the device to operate on.
  7. Enter your SmartSwitch username.
